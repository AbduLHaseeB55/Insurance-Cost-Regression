# 🏥 Healthcare Analytics: Medical Insurance Cost Regression

## 🎯 Business Objective
The insurance industry relies on precise mathematical risk assessment. This project analyzes patient demographics (Age, BMI, Smoking status) to determine medical cost drivers and predicts annual charges for new patients for dynamic pricing.

## 📊 Key Business Insights
* **Primary Drivers:** Smoking status and BMI are the most significant factors affecting medical costs.
* **Predictive Accuracy:** The model successfully captures complex, non-linear pricing structures.

## 🤖 Machine Learning Model
* **Algorithm:** Random Forest Regressor
* **Performance:** 86.5% R-squared score
* **Application:** Automated, data-driven insurance premium calculation.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Python** (Pandas, Scikit-Learn)
* **Visualization:** Seaborn, Matplotlib
